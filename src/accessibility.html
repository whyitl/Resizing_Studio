Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 25, 2026

01

Our commitment

Resizing Studio is committed to making www.resizing.ca accessible to as many people as possible, regardless of ability or technology. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

02

What we do

We build accessibility into how the Site is made:

  • Semantic structure: pages use proper heading hierarchy and landmark elements so screen readers can navigate them logically.
  • Keyboard support: interactive elements, including expandable work and FAQ sections, can be operated with the keyboard.
  • Media: meaningful images include text alternatives; decorative visuals are hidden from assistive technologies.
  • Visual clarity: we use a consistent type and color system designed for readable contrast across light and dark sections.
  • Responsive design: the Site adapts to different screen sizes and zoom levels.
03

Known limitations

We're continually improving, and some areas may not yet fully meet our target:

  • Decorative and animated elements are non-essential; if any are difficult to interact with, all key content and actions remain available elsewhere on the page.
  • Some third-party embedded content may not fully match our accessibility standards, as it is controlled by external providers.

If you encounter any of these, please tell us — see below.

04

Feedback and help

If you have trouble accessing any part of the Site, or need information in a different format, we want to help. Contact us:

  • Email: contact@resizing.ca

We aim to respond within 5 business days and will work with you to provide the information or service you need.

05

Ongoing work

We review accessibility as part of our development process and update this statement as the Site evolves. This statement was last reviewed on June 25, 2026.
