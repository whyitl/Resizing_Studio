FREQUENTLY ASKED

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects are completed within 4-5 weeks depending on scope and how quickly feedback is provided. Larger or more complex sites may take longer.

Do you offer ongoing support or maintenance?

Yes. We offer maintenance packages to keep your site secure, up-to-date, and running smoothly after launch. Ask us about our ongoing support options.

What if I need changes after the website goes live?

Minor revisions within the agreed scope are included. For additional changes after launch, we offer affordable hourly or retainer-based support.

Who hosts the website?

Your website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, connected directly to a GitHub repository. Whenever updates are made, we push the changes to GitHub and Cloudflare automatically detects the commit, builds the project, and deploys it live — all within minutes.
