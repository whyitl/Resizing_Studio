Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

Resizing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us. Please read this policy carefully to understand how we handle your information.

01

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes data you provide directly, such as your name, business name, email address, and any other details you submit through our contact forms or other website interactions.
  • Usage Data: This includes information collected automatically, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. We collect this data through Cloudflare Web Analytics.
02

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Respond to inquiries submitted through our contact forms
  • Analyze site usage to improve functionality and user experience
03

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use Google Analytics. We may use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate traffic (for example, pages viewed and approximate location derived from IP at a regional level). That product is designed to operate without tracking cookies for visitors. Your browser may still store small amounts of data for essential security or delivery (for example, from our hosting provider); those are not used by us for marketing or profiling.

Some pages include an embedded Spotify playlist player. When that player loads or you interact with it, Spotify may set its own cookies or similar technologies on your device (for example, to remember preferences or measure usage). We do not control those cookies. For details, see Spotify's Privacy Policy.

If we introduce additional non-essential cookies or similar technologies on our site (for example, marketing or advanced analytics that require consent in your province), we will ask for your permission before they run, where required by law.

04

How We Protect Your Data

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information, including encryption and secure servers. However, no online method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

05

Privacy Officer

For questions about this policy or how we handle personal information—including requests under Canadian privacy law—you may contact our Privacy Officer at the email below. The Privacy Officer is responsible for overseeing our privacy practices and responding to access and correction requests where applicable.

Email: contact@resizing.ca

06

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We may share information only with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help operate our website and services (for example, website hosting and analytics as described above). They are required to keep your data secure and confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, or to respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
07

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict how we use your data
  • Withdraw consent for future processing

To exercise your rights, contact us at contact@resizing.ca

08

Third-Party Links

Our website may link to other sites we don't control, or include embedded content from third parties (such as the Spotify player in our footer). We're not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites or providers. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information or interacting with embedded content.

09

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with a new "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, feel free to reach out:

Email: contact@resizing.ca
